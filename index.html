Three Pillars of My Journey

My life is built upon three foundational pillars that shape my purpose and drive my passion.

Conscious Business

I hold a degree in Economics and Commerce, a specialization in Foreign Trade, and an MBA. I am also a licensed real estate agent in Italy. I have a deep passion for the Croatian market - my homeland - and I promote real estate investments there, especially in the tourism and hospitality sectors.

What Conscious Business Means to Me:

For me, conscious business goes beyond profit - it's about creating a positive impact in the world. I believe that success should be measured not only by financial gains, but by the lives we touch and the communities we uplift.

A portion of every business venture I engage in is dedicated to social causes close to my heart. I actively support orphanages, contribute to educational initiatives for underprivileged children, and believe in using business as a force for good.

"True prosperity flows when we lift others as we rise. Every deal, every investment, every success carries within it the seeds of service to humanity."

ART by Simona

A Journey of Soul and Color

My art is a spiritual expression — born from intuition, emotion, and a deep connection with nature and the Divine. I work with mixed techniques, including resin, natural pigments, sacred symbols, and layered textures to awaken something ancient in the viewer's heart.

Each piece is a portal — an invitation to feel, to remember, and to return to wholeness.

My creations reflect themes of:

  • Sacred geometry
  • Elements and seasons
  • Feminine energy and inner strength
  • Ocean, trees, and the golden light of the soul

"Through color and texture, I channel the whispers of the soul, creating bridges between the seen and unseen worlds. Each brushstroke is a prayer, each layer a meditation."

Spiritual Path

My Christian spiritual journey has taken me to sacred places around the world, each pilgrimage deepening my understanding of faith and purpose.

Coming from Croatia, where Catholic faith remains a vibrant part of our European heritage, I carry deep spiritual traditions that have guided my footsteps along ancient pilgrimage paths.

My journey has taken me to Santiago de Compostela along the sacred Camino, to the mystical Mont Saint-Michel rising from French waters, and to Italy's San Michele Sacred Sites where the Archangel's protection can be felt. In Jerusalem, the holy city where our faith was born, my understanding of Christianity's roots deepened, while in Assisi I walked in Saint Francis' footsteps, discovering profound simplicity and surrender to God's will.

"These sacred pilgrimages have woven together to form the foundation of my spiritual path, connecting my Croatian heritage with the universal call to seek the divine, bridging tradition with my inner and outer mission."
